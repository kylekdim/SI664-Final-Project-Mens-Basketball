About Basketball All-Stars

This Django site is built on a MySQL database called "allstars" containing records of people and teams associated with American professional basketball leagues from 1937 to 2011. In addition to well-known players and teams from the NBA, the database also contains player, team, and all-star records for more obscure leagues, such as the ABA.
